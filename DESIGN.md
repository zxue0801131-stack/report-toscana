## Style Prompt

Create a LoveFrom-inspired animated PowerPoint cover for a Tuscan natural luxury villa proposal. The canvas is warm near-white plaster, the typography is black-brown serif, and the experience relies on silence, space, precise type, image placeholders, and micro-motion. The mood is Apple/Jony Ive restraint translated into Tuscan materiality: limewash, walnut, travertine, linen, wool, old trees, courtyard fire, pool light, and collected objects.

## Colors

- Background plaster: `#faf8f1`
- Primary ink: `#080806`
- Soft ink: `rgba(8, 8, 6, 0.48)`
- Hairline ink: `rgba(8, 8, 6, 0.18)`
- Warm shadow: `rgba(55, 39, 22, 0.08)`

## Typography

- Primary serif: `EB Garamond`, `Garamond`, `Georgia`, serif
- Body serif: `EB Garamond`, `Georgia`, serif
- Use elegant, humanist serif type with natural tracking. Avoid sans-serif UI except hidden accessibility labels.

## Motion

- Slow, precise, quiet motion.
- Typewriter reveal for the main wordmark, followed by PPT cover metadata.
- Comma behaves like a small animated calligraphic mark: blink, breathe, and subtly deform.
- Three quiet image placeholders are reserved for future client imagery: garden/pool/old trees, material detail, and furniture scene.
- Reduced-motion users see immediate, still states.

## What NOT to Do

- No gradients, cards, drop shadows, decorative blobs, stock photos, or hero illustrations.
- No marketing buttons, nav bars, badges, or feature labels.
- No bright color accents.
- No shiny traditional luxury cues such as crystal chandeliers or gold overload.
- No busy layout; the first screen should feel almost empty.
